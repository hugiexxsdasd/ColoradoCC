COLORADO ROLEPLAY

Colorado Roleplay is built to bring people together through immersive storytelling, shared scenes, and collaborative world-building. Every member uses the server differently — some stick to tight-knit friend groups, others participate in large public RP spaces, and many move fluidly between both.

Across Colorado Roleplay, we design our systems, rules, and staff practices with privacy and transparency in mind. We want you to always understand how your information, your characters, and your interactions are handled.

GUIDELINES

OUR GUIDELINES

Welcome to Colorado Roleplay Guidelines.

As stated in our Discord server, our guidelines are extremely important for the wellbeing of not only our team, but also our community members who support us.

By joining our server, you agree to follow our guidelines and agree not to break them.

Breaking our guidelines will result in consequences, which may include moderation action.

Please be mindful of what you say and do, and always consider how your words and actions may affect the wellbeing of others.

Treat everyone with respect. No toxic behavior, insults, discrimination, or hostility. Keep disagreements calm, and keep feedback constructive.
NSFW content is strictly prohibited. This includes explicit images, sexual content, graphic media, or links to NSFW material.
Raiding is not allowed. Do not coordinate spam, attacks, harassment, or disruption against Colorado Roleplay or any other community.
Harassment, bullying, threats, hate, or stalking may result in immediate moderation action. Respect boundaries and stop when asked.
