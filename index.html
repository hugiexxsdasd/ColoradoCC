COLORADO CC — WELCOME

Colorado Roleplay is built to bring people together through immersive storytelling, shared scenes, and collaborative world-building. Every member uses the server differently—some stick to tight-knit friend groups, others participate in large public RP spaces, and many move fluidly between both. Because of that, expectations of privacy naturally shift depending on where you are in the server.

Across Colorado Roleplay, we design our systems, rules, and staff practices with privacy and transparency in mind. We want you to always understand how your information, your characters, and your interactions are handled.
