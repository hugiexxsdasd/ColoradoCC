WELCOME TO LOTUS CUSTOMS

Lotus Customs is a creative space built for people who love intentional design. Whether you’re refining your brand, building a server aesthetic, or exploring new visual styles, our community gives you the tools and support to grow. We’re committed to making design feel welcoming, accessible, and genuinely enjoyable. In the Lotus Knowledge Centre, you’ll find resources for every kind of creator — from beginners experimenting with their first palette to experienced designers shaping full visual identities.

OUR GUIDELINES

Lotus Customs presents its navigation in a clean, intentional layout that mirrors the precision of the design work the community is known for. At the top of the interface, visitors find a row of categories that feel both minimal and expressive, each one crafted to guide users toward the resources they need without overwhelming them. The structure is simple, but the visual language is unmistakably Lotus: soft edges, balanced spacing, and a subtle emphasis on clarity.

The section inspired by the “Support” tab in the image becomes a polished, design-centric hub for creators. When someone hovers over it, a dropdown unfolds with smooth motion, revealing a set of curated options that feel like part of a cohesive brand system. Instead of generic labels, each item is written with intention, helping users understand exactly where to go for help, feedback, or guidance. The dropdown itself uses a soft pink accent, matching Lotus Customs’ aesthetic and reinforcing the idea that support is part of the creative experience.
